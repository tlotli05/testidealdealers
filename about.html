"

Ideal Dealers was founded from a personal journey of ambition, frustration, and discovery. The founder, Lekobe Makgaretsa, had a strong vision to build a business but initially had little knowledge of where to begin. The process was challenging and often frustrating — requiring patience, learning, and persistence. But every step proved to be worth it.

Throughout this journey, Lekobe developed a deep passion and natural knack for creating business ideas. To turn vision into knowledge, he pursued marketing management and business development courses through Udemy, gaining practical insight into running and growing a business. With this new understanding came a powerful realisation: many aspiring entrepreneurs face the same struggles — wasting time trying to figure everything out alone.

From that realisation, Ideal Dealers was born. The purpose is simple: to help entrepreneurs and businesses build their dream brands without the confusion and delays that often come with starting or growing a business. By handling branding, marketing, and digital development, Ideal Dealers allows business owners to focus on what matters most — running and growing their business.

— Lekobe Makgaretsa, CEO & Founder
